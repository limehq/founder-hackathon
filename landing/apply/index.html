QUESTIONS, ANSWERED

Cooking for income — the honest FAQ

The legal and practical questions every cook asks before joining.

Do I need a registered business (Gewerbe)?
To accept paid orders today, yes — a Gewerbe and a compliant kitchen. No business yet? Join the Pathway and we help you get there.
What about hygiene rules?
You confirm IfSG §43 food-safety training and a short kitchen self-disclosure as part of the Verified Gate.
How and when do I get paid?
Fixed price per order, set up front. Caterists invoices the client and pays you out after delivery — no chasing.
What does it cost me?
€0 to join. We take a flat 12% on confirmed orders. Optional Pro tools later — never pay to get matched.
Do I have to take every order?
No. Only orders matching your capacity, region and lead time reach you — accept the ones you want.
I only cook from home. Can I join?
Not as a live caterer yet — but the Pathway (hygiene, business coaching, partner cloud-kitchen hours) is built for exactly that.
